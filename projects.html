Piggy

Your Ultimate Financial Companion: Manage Expenses & Earnings with Piggy!

Take control of your personal finances effortlessly with Piggy, the intuitive and elegant app designed to simplify expense and earnings tracking. Say goodbye to financial guesswork and hello to seamless money management. Piggy's goal is to provide a user-friendly experience with a beautiful interface, making financial tracking a breeze. The app presents a clean, welcoming interface, inviting you to take charge of your financial health.

Features

  • Elegant User Interface

    Built upon Google's modern Material 3 design system, Piggy boasts a clean, beautiful, and intuitive user interface. The app's design emphasizes clarity and aesthetic appeal for a pleasant tracking experience.

  • User-Friendly Experience

    Enjoy a clutter-free and seamless user experience from the moment you create an account to generating comprehensive financial reports. Piggy is designed for simplicity, ensuring anyone can easily manage their finances without a steep learning curve.

  • Secure Cloud Data Storage

    Never worry about losing your financial data again! Thanks to the robust Google Firebase APIs, Piggy securely stores all your data in the cloud. This means you can effortlessly transfer your financial records to another device and easily log in using your existing Google account for quick and secure access.

New Year Countdown

Get ready to ring in the New Year with a dazzling display and an exciting countdown experience! The New Year Countdown app transforms your device into a festive hub, helping you anticipate the big moment.

This app features a prominent and clear countdown to the start of the new year, ensuring you won't miss a second of the excitement. As the clock ticks down, you'll be greeted with a vibrant welcome message, followed by a heartfelt "HAPPY NEW YEAR" as the fireworks burst on screen, reminiscent of a lively 3-minute countdown video. The festive background, alive with colorful, pixel-like fireworks, sets the perfect celebratory mood.

Whether you're celebrating with friends and family or enjoying a quiet moment, New Year Countdown is your perfect companion to usher in the new year with joy and visual spectacle.

Features

  • New Year Greetings

    See "HAPPY NEW YEAR" displayed prominently as the countdown concludes.

  • Engaging Experience

High Horizon Hotel Manager

Optimize Your Hotel Operations with High Horizon Hotel Manager!

Take control of your hotel's daily operations with High Horizon Hotel Manager, the intuitive and efficient solution designed to streamline room management. Whether you're overseeing a single building or an entire chain, this app provides a clear, real-time overview of your room statuses, ensuring smooth check-ins, efficient housekeeping, and prompt maintenance.

The user-friendly interface allows you to effortlessly monitor and manage every room in your property. Say goodbye to manual tracking and welcome a new era of digital efficiency!

Features

  • Real-time Room Status Overview

    Instantly see the current state of all rooms, categorized by "Open," "Booked," "Occupied," "Housekeeping," and "Maintenance".

  • Intuitive Room Display

    Rooms are clearly laid out by level (e.g., Level 1, Level 2, Level 3) within each building, making navigation simple and quick.

  • Visual Status Indicators

    Each room (e.g., A301, A201, A101) is color-coded, providing an immediate visual cue of its status (e.g., red for occupied, green for open).

  • Building Selection

    Easily switch between different buildings or towers within your hotel property to manage rooms effectively.

  • Room Details at a Glance

    See essential information like room numbers (e.g., A301, A201) and room types (e.g., "Double Room," "Twin Room," "Single Room") directly on the main screen.

  • Streamlined Operations

    Facilitates quick identification of rooms needing attention, improving staff response times and guest satisfaction.

Pong: Legends

Relive the classic arcade action with Pong: Legends! This homage to one of gaming's foundational titles brings the timeless paddle-and-ball challenge to your device, offering a pure, unadulterated retro experience. Perfect for quick breaks or competitive showdowns, Pong: Legends delivers simple yet addictive gameplay that stands the test of time.

  • Retro Visuals

    Enjoy the authentic black and white, minimalist design reminiscent of the original arcade game.

  • Competitive Challenge

    Engage in fast-paced rallies, testing your reflexes and strategic thinking against an opponent.

  • Accessible Design

    Designed to be easy to pick up and play for all ages, while offering a challenge for mastery.
