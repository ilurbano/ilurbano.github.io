About

Ivan Louise Urbano

Hello! I’m Ivan, a BSIT-MWA student with a passion for programming, particularly in Android app and web development, and I enjoy creating interactive and user-friendly applications. I also enjoy turning ideas into digital solutions. Whether it’s through sleek frontend interfaces or thoughtful backend logic.

Outside of coding, I’m always exploring ways to grow emotionally and intellectually, balancing tech skills with people skills. Currently building my portfolio, one project and one lesson at a time. In my free time, I love to cook and explore new recipes. Cooking is not just a hobby for me; it's a way to express creativity and share joy with others. I also enjoy playing video games, which helps me relax and unwind after a long day of coding.
